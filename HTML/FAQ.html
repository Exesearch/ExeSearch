What is ExeSearch?

 ExeSearch is a treasure hunt game designed to help new students find their way around campus and meet new people. Students are sorted into groups by tutors. Then given clues to follow and tasks to complete in order to find their way to the final treasure. Along the way they gain points by getting to the correct location and asnwering the questions correctly.

How do I play the game?

 To play the game all members of the group needs to be in the meeting location and each member of the group has to press the start button in your profile pages. Then you are taken to the quiz page where the game commences. You answer a question which gives you a clue to a location.You will need to walk to that location with your group,then click a button to check your group position. If you are in the right location, your group will be awarded points and then you will be given the next question to answer.This repeats until you have completed the treasure hunt. If unable to answer questions you are given a hint towards the location which you have to head to. However your group doesn't recieve any points.

I'm lost! Please help.

 If you are lost, load up Google maps. If you need immediate help, phone the campus Estate Patrol on 01392 723999, or if it's extremely serious, please call 999.

How do I see my group score?

 You can see your group score in the score board page and every time you play a game the score just keeps increasing.

How do I find out what group I am in?

 You can find out what group you are in by looking at your profile page where it will contain your group name. This was assigned by your tutors.

Do I have to remain with my group?

 Please ensure you remain with your team members while playing the game. If you do not want to continue playing please let other team members know and they can keep continuing to play the game.
